Understanding Calorie Intake

Calories are units of energy. When you eat or drink, you're giving your body calories. Your body uses this energy for everything â€” from breathing to lifting weights.

How Many Calories Do You Need?

This depends on your age, gender, weight, activity level, and fitness goals:

  • To lose weight: Eat fewer calories than your body uses.
  • To maintain weight: Balance the calories you eat and burn.
  • To gain weight/muscle: Eat more calories with a focus on protein.

Good Sources of Calories

  • Protein: Chicken, eggs, tofu, fish
  • Carbs: Brown rice, oats, sweet potatoes
  • Fats: Avocado, nuts, olive oil

Tracking Calories

Use apps like MyFitnessPal to track your daily intake and match it with your fitness goals. Consistency is key!
